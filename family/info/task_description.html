Michael always knew that there was something wrong with his family. Many strangers were introduced to him as part of his family.

Michael should figure this out. He spent almost a month parsing family archives. He has all father-son connections of his entire family collected in one place.

With all that data Michel can easily understand who the strangers are. Or maybe the only stranger in this family is Michael? Let’ see.

You have a list of family relationships between father and son. Every element on this list each has two elements. First is father's name, second is son’s name. All names in the family are unique. Check if famaly tree is correct. There are no stranges in the family tree. All connections in family are natural.

Input: list of list. Every element has two strings. List has at least one element

Output: bool. Is famaly tree correct.
